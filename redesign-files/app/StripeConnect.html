Payment settings

How Blawby processes payments through your Stripe account.

Auto-charge retainer replenishments
When a retainer drops below the threshold, charge the card on file automatically. Client receives a receipt.
Trust deposit routing
Route trust deposits to your IOLTA account automatically. Requires a separate bank account in Stripe.
Payment link branding
Client-facing payment pages show your firm name and logo, not "Blawby".
Require invoice before payment
Clients can only pay after an invoice is generated. Disabling allows ad-hoc payment links.

Disconnect Stripe

Disconnecting stops all payment processing. Outstanding invoices will show as unpaid. Trust ledger entries are preserved.
